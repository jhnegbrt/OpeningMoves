Welcome to Opening Moves!

Investor & Trading Research

Motivation:

Stock price volatility immediately following the stock-market 'open' (9:30 EST) is commonly understood as an important indicator of a number of intra-day price patterns.

Attempting to capitalize on these pattenrs, investors and traders may ask: how often does a stock's price change 1% within the first 5 minutes of the day? Within the first 10 minutes of the day?

And perhaps more importantly... what do these changes indicate about the behavior of the stock over the rest of the day?

Opening Moves is designed to help you answer these questions.

Note on data and x-axes:

The price data API used for this project occassionally returns incomplete data-sets, which may impact the quality of research done using this tool.

When an incomplete dataset is returned, you will notice that the x-axis of the corresponding graph has missing ticks where data is missing.
